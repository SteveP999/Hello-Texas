About

Hello Texas

★

Hello Texas is a Texas Country band that sounds exactly like where they're from — big stages, open roads, and the kind of songs that hit different when you're driving home late with the windows down.

They write about real things: loyalty, loss, the weight of silence, the pull of home. No irony, no hedging. Just Texas country music played the way it's supposed to be played — loud enough to mean it, honest enough to stick.

Released through Hello Texas Records, their growing catalog of singles spans everything from anthem-sized crowd raisers to quiet gut-punches. Every song earns its place.
